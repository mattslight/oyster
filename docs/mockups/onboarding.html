Design notes

  • Terminology discipline: step 1 says "Connect your agent", never "Connect your AI". The AI brain (OpenCode/provider) is step 0, handled at CLI.
  • Step 2 hierarchy: folder drop is primary, agent-prompt is a secondary link under the zone. Never shown at equal weight.
  • Step 3 framing: optional in the step title, trust-first copy above the CTA, green-tinted note reinforces privacy.
  • Completion labels (e.g. "· 3 spaces", "· Claude Code") give the user feedback their action registered.
  • Header copy changes with progress: value prop → optional-nudge → set-up confirmation.
  • Dismissal: checklist collapses to a tiny "show onboarding" row. Users who power past the guide get their surface back immediately.
  • Completion detection (story A7) is polish — a manual "I've connected it" button is the fallback if SSE/poll detection is flaky.
