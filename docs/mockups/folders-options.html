Why this lands:
  • Top nav never moves. Same pills, same position, every screen. Only the highlight changes.
  • Content always shows children-of-current-scope as its first section. Home → Spaces. A space → Projects. Same primitive (tile grid) at every level — different children.
  • Naming is honest. Tiles say Projects, not Folders, because a project IS the unit of scope (which happens to be backed by a linked folder OR the userland scratchpad).
  • Quick-move preserved. Top pills give one-click jump to any space. No more "I have no way back."
  • Chat-bar pills at the bottom keep doing their job (typing-context scope) but they're no longer the only nav surface — top is the canonical one for orientation.
What changes vs. now:
  • Breadcrumb pills become the universal top nav — render on Home too (with Home highlighted).
  • Home's existing space-cards grid stays, but framed as a content section called Spaces — not as "the nav."
  • Home/Elsewhere only appear in the top pills, not as content cards — they're nav-level concepts, not real-space children.
  • Project tiles section gets the heading Projects on a space (instead of nothing).
