Folder chip behaviour
  • Click chip → grid scopes to that folder. Tiles from other folders dim out (or hide; toggle below).
  • Click selected chip again → clear. Or click Clear ✕ in the filter bar above the grid.
  • Hover chip → red ✕ appears top-right. Click → confirm modal → soft-delete the source + its artefacts. Reattach restores everything.
  • Green pip on a chip = a live (active or waiting) claude session in that folder's cwd. The "we care because claude is operating on it" signal, no extra click needed.
  • ★ = primary (namesake match → fallback to oldest attached). Filled chip styling reinforces it.
  • + attach chip → inline form same as today.

Detach folder?

Its artefacts will be hidden. Reattach the same path to restore them.
