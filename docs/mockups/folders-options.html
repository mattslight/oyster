Try it:
  • Click oyster-os → grid filters · click again or Clear ✕ → all back
  • Click + attach chip → opens the manage list (and could focus its input)
  • Click ▸ Manage folders → expands the audit list with paths, attach times, Detach buttons
  • Click Detach on a row → confirm modal
What this trades:
  • One chip click = one thing (filter). No accidental destruction.
  • Detach takes two deliberate clicks (expand → detach button → confirm). Right velocity for a destructive op.
  • Power users keep the simple list view. Casual users see clean chips.
  • Disclosure persists in localStorage — open once, stays open.
