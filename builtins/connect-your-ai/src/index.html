Bring Your Own AI.

Oyster is an MCP server. Any AI that speaks the protocol can control your workspace.

Quick start
run in your terminal 

        

        

        

      

    


    
What you can do

    

      

        "Create a deck about Q2 results"
        →
        appears on your surface
      

      

        "Scan ~/Dev/my-project"
        →
        new space with apps, docs, and diagrams
      

      

        "Open the competitor analysis"
        →
        opens it in the viewer
      

      

        "Move the pitch deck to clients"
        →
        reassigned instantly
      

      

        "Remember the deadline is Friday"
        →
        stored in memory, recalled later
