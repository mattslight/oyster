Bring Your Own AI

Oyster is an MCP server. Any AI that speaks the protocol can control your workspace.

Quick start

run in your terminal

      


      

        
.cursor/mcp.json

        

      


      

        
.vscode/mcp.json

        

      


      

        
~/.codeium/windsurf/mcp_config.json

        

      

    


    
What you can do

    

      

        "Create a deck about Q2 results"
        →
        appears on your surface
      

      

        "Scan ~/Dev/my-project"
        →
        new space with apps, docs, and diagrams discovered
      

      

        "Open the competitor analysis"
        →
        opens it in the viewer
      

      

        "Move the pitch deck to the clients space"
        →
        reassigned instantly
      

      

        "Remember the deadline is Friday"
        →
        stored in memory, recalled later
      

    


    
Available tools

    

      create_artifact
      list_artifacts
      read_artifact
      update_artifact
      open_artifact
      remove_artifact
      onboard_space
      scan_space
      list_spaces
      switch_space
      remember
      recall
      gather_repo_context
      regenerate_icon
    

    
Your AI can chain these together. Ask it to onboard a project, scan for artifacts, create a summary, and organise everything into spaces — all from one prompt.
